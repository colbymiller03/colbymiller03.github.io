Vehicle Overview

Theseus

April 2026

Beach Launch Team's first liquid rocket launch in six years.

Theseus is a pressure regulated, pressure fed liquid rocket developed by Beach Launch Team at California State University, Long Beach. The vehicle uses liquid oxygen and RP1 to power a regeneratively cooled LR101 rocket engine.

During the 2025 to 2026 development campaign, I served as Assistant Fluids Lead and Propulsion Member. My work focused on manufacturing and integrating flight plumbing, executing system tests, supporting cryogenic operations, and preparing the vehicle for static fire and launch.

The project provided direct experience working with a complete liquid propulsion system from hardware fabrication through integrated vehicle operations.
